My passions

GRAPHIC DESIGN

I have always been a creative soul;
as a kid I would use hours just pouring my ideas into paper. Now my pen is a digital one and my toolbox ever-expanding. With Creative Cloud as my canvas, I'm able to create using Photoshop, Illustrator and After Effects. I dream of developing these skills more in a future job.

THE CREATIVE PROCESS

If a creative process were a cake, I would love to be one of the bakers.
I'm most experienced with the "Design Thinking" recipe, but I believe that a "delicious cake" consists of good planning (maybe with a sprinkle of SCRUM), great teamwork, a dash of decorating skills and, of course, taste testing through UI/UX tests.

COMBINING CODE AND DESIGN

An architect usually can't build a house without craftsmen - but to create the best solutions, they need to understand each other at some level. The same thing goes for web designers and developers. I wish to use my experience in coding to function as a link between design and development.

... OUTSIDE THE SCREEN

I like to go for long walks in either city or nature to find inspiration in my surroundings. When hanging out with friends I’m always up for playing games whether it’s board games or video games, visiting cozy bars, or having sweet night-ins.
